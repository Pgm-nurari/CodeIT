Lists in HTML

There are basically 3 types of list structures in html.

Ordered List

  1. Monday and tuesday
  2. Wednesday and Thursday
  3. January and March
  4. November and December

Unordered List

  • Monday and tuesday
  • Wednesday and Thursday
  • January and March
  • November and December

Descriptive List

Weeks:
Monday and tuesday
Wednesday and Thursday
Months:
January and March
November and December
